## When to Choose Our Solution

Our self-hosted GDPR Cookie Solution is ideal for:

1. **Budget-conscious organizations**: If you want to avoid ongoing subscription costs.
2. **Developers and technical teams**: If you have the technical resources to implement and maintain it.
3. **Privacy-focused companies**: If you want full control over your data and don't want third-party access to user consent.
4. **Customization needs**: If you require deep customization beyond what paid services offer.
5. **Integration with existing systems**: If you need to tightly integrate with your own infrastructure.

## When to Choose a Paid Solution

Paid solutions like Cookiebot may be better suited for:

1. **Non-technical users**: If you don't have development resources available.
2. **Legal assurance**: If you want a service with legal teams that monitor regulatory changes.
3. **Enterprise needs**: If you require advanced features like A/B testing or enterprise-level support.
4. **Complex multi-domain setups**: If you manage many different websites across various domains.
5. **Time constraints**: If you need an immediate solution without development time.

## Making the Decision

Consider these questions when deciding between our self-hosted solution and a paid service:

1. **Budget**: How much are you willing to spend on cookie compliance?
2. **Technical resources**: Do you have developers who can implement and maintain the solution?
3. **Customization needs**: Do you need deep customization of the cookie banner and consent forms?
4. **Legal requirements**: How critical is compliance with the latest regulations for your business?
5. **Scale**: How many websites do you need to manage?
6. **Time**: Do you need an immediate solution or can you invest time in implementation?

## Hybrid Approach

You might also consider a hybrid approach:

1. Start with our self-hosted solution to minimize costs
2. Upgrade to a paid service if and when:
   - Your website scales significantly
   - You need advanced features
   - Regulations change in ways that are difficult to implement yourself
   - You no longer have the technical resources to maintain the solution
